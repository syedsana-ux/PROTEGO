About Portego

Who We Are

Portego is a safety‑focused digital platform created to support individuals experiencing domestic violence, emotional abuse, or unsafe environments. We provide discreet, accessible, and reliable tools designed to help users seek help safely and privately.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower survivors with confidential resources, immediate access to help, and the knowledge they need to stay safe. Domestic violence often goes unseen — Portego exists to ensure no one has to face danger alone.

  • Provide fast access to nearby shelters and support centers
  • Offer a safe and anonymous communication channel
  • Educate users on creating safety plans
  • Enable secure reporting with privacy-first design

Why This Exists

Millions of people experience domestic violence every year, yet many do not feel safe seeking help openly. Portego was built to bridge this gap — offering immediate, discreet support and guidance in moments when safety matters most.

Safety Instructions

Your safety comes first. Please keep these instructions in mind while using Portego:

  • Use the Quick Exit button if you feel someone is watching.
  • Avoid saving website history; use incognito or private browsing if possible.
  • Do not share personal information unless you feel completely safe.
  • Use neutral names to save emergency contacts.
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels unsafe, act immediately.
